System and Processes

Hospital Supportive services include efficient systems, a systematic process driven approach, advanced technology, advanced rehabilitative services and after care.Nurses and technicians from every corner of the country. Our infrastructure design is compliant with NABH and JCI.


Quality

Quality at Hitha covers various aspects of patient care, patient safety, care coordination and patient engagement. Standardised protocols and practices have been set for infection control, emergency services and critical care. Our quality aspiration is to have zero errors.


Nursing

Our nursing protocols are defined by blending the most ethical and advanced of Indian nursing practices.Infection control, medication management & patient safety are the most important components for our nursing care.
